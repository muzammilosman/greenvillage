About Us

Home About Us

Green Village is an NGO registered under Societies Act and comprise of a group of socially committed professionals who are actively involved in Municipal Solid Waste Management for promoting Green Protocol and Green Protocol Based Source Level Waste Management. As per the Kerala State Government Order Number 2700/2017 LSGD, Dated 04.08.2017, Green Village is an authorized service provider of Suchitwa Mission for implementing Green Protocol for institutions and events. As per the Kerala State Government Order Number H. K. M. /A2/625/18, Dated 27.03.2018; Green Village is one of the Technical Consultant authorized for providing technical assistance for Local Self Governments across Kerala for implementing Decentralized Solid Waste Management.

Vision

To inculcate the idea “My Waste, My Responsibility” by focusing on Green Protocol Based Source Level Waste Management and providing sustainable solutions for its implementation

Mission

Strive towards building an eco-friendly environment through promotion of sustainable source level waste management solutions and execution of green protocol. Provide scientific, technical and viable assistance for executing Green Protocol Based Source Level Waste Management in Local Self Governments, Government and Semi-Government Organizations, Private Institutions and Events.
